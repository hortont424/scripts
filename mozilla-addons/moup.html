The Live Bookmarks feature of Firefox is great, but it's missing a way to quickly access a Live Bookmark's home page without having to create an additional bookmark or go through lots of extra clicks. LiveClick fixes this oversight. All you have to do is specify a location in the Live Bookmark's properties.
